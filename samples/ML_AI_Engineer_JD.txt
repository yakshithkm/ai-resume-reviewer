MACHINE LEARNING/AI ENGINEER JOB DESCRIPTION

Position: Senior ML/AI Engineer
Location: Remote / Boston, MA
Experience Level: 5-7 years

COMPANY OVERVIEW
IntelliAI Technologies is a leader in artificial intelligence solutions. We develop cutting-edge machine learning systems for enterprise customers spanning healthcare, finance, retail, and technology sectors.

POSITION OVERVIEW
We're seeking a talented ML/AI Engineer to design and deploy production machine learning systems. You'll work on challenging problems impacting millions of users and have the opportunity to shape AI strategy.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
• Design and develop machine learning models and systems
• Build end-to-end ML pipelines from data to production
• Fine-tune and deploy large language models
• Implement computer vision and NLP solutions
• Optimize model performance and inference latency
• Build feature engineering and data processing pipelines
• Create evaluation frameworks and metrics
• Implement MLOps and model monitoring systems
• Collaborate with data engineers and product teams
• Conduct experimentation and A/B testing

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
• 5+ years of machine learning or data science experience
• Expert-level Python proficiency
• Strong foundation in statistics and algorithms
• Experience with deep learning frameworks (TensorFlow or PyTorch)
• Proven ability to deploy ML models to production
• Experience with SQL and data manipulation
• Understanding of ML best practices and evaluation metrics
• Knowledge of data preprocessing and feature engineering
• Strong problem-solving and communication skills
• Portfolio of ML projects or contributions

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
• Experience with large language models and fine-tuning
• Expertise in computer vision or natural language processing
• Knowledge of MLOps and model deployment (Docker, Kubernetes)
• Experience with distributed training and big data tools
• AWS SageMaker or Google Cloud ML experience
• Publication of research or contributions to ML community
• Experience with reinforcement learning
• Knowledge of model optimization and compression techniques
• Familiarity with recommendation systems
• Advanced degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, or related field
