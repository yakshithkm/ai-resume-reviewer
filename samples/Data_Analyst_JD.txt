DATA ANALYST JOB DESCRIPTION

Position: Senior Data Analyst
Location: Remote / New York, NY
Experience Level: 4-6 years

COMPANY OVERVIEW
Analytics Pro Corp is a leading business intelligence consulting firm serving 500+ enterprise clients. We help organizations leverage data to drive business strategy and improve decision making.

POSITION OVERVIEW
We're looking for a talented Data Analyst to join our Analytics team. You'll partner with clients and internal stakeholders to identify insights, build dashboards, and drive data-informed decisions.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
• Extract, transform, and analyze large datasets using SQL and Python
• Design and build interactive dashboards and visualizations in Tableau/Power BI
• Conduct statistical analysis and hypothesis testing to validate business assumptions
• Develop and maintain ETL pipelines for data processing
• Create data models and documentation for stakeholders
• Identify data quality issues and implement solutions
• Present insights and recommendations to business leaders
• Conduct A/B testing and experimental design
• Monitor KPIs and create performance dashboards
• Collaborate with product, engineering, and business teams

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
• 4-6 years of data analysis experience
• Expert-level SQL proficiency for complex queries
• Strong proficiency in Python or R for data manipulation and analysis
• Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or similar)
• Understanding of statistical concepts and methods
• Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL)
• Knowledge of Excel (advanced functions and pivot tables)
• Strong communication and presentation skills
• Ability to translate business questions into analytical solutions
• Problem-solving mindset with attention to detail

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
• Experience with cloud data warehouses (Redshift, BigQuery, Snowflake)
• Knowledge of Apache Spark or other big data tools
• Experience with ETL tools (Talend, Airflow, etc.)
• Familiarity with machine learning concepts
• Experience with version control (Git)
• Knowledge of A/B testing and experimental design
• Experience with NoSQL databases
• Proficiency in R
• Dashboard and report automation skills
• Business acumen and domain knowledge

TECHNICAL SKILLS REQUIRED
Databases: PostgreSQL, MySQL, or other relational databases
Languages: Python or R (expert level), SQL (expert level)
BI Tools: Tableau, Power BI, or Google Data Studio
Excel: Advanced proficiency

COMPENSATION & BENEFITS
• Competitive salary: $90,000 - $130,000 annually
• Performance bonus
• Health, dental, vision insurance
• 401(k) matching
• Flexible work schedule
• Professional development budget
• Remote work flexibility

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR
An analytical thinker who's passionate about data and can communicate findings to non-technical audiences. You should be self-motivated, curious, and comfortable with ambiguity. We value continuous learning and experimentation.

APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
• Portfolio with data analysis projects and visualizations
• Examples of dashboards or reports you've created
• Understanding of business metrics and KPIs
• Clear explanation of your data analysis approach

EQUAL OPPORTUNITY
We're committed to building a diverse and inclusive team.
